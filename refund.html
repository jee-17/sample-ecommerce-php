Conditions for Returns

Returns are only applicable if the order is cancelled before it has been shipped. Once the order is shipped, we are unable to accept returns or issue refunds.

The following Goods cannot be returned under any circumstances:

  • Goods made to Your specifications or clearly personalized.
  • Goods that deteriorate rapidly or where the expiry date is over.
  • Unsealed Goods unsuitable for health protection or hygiene reasons.
  • Goods inseparably mixed with other items after delivery.

We reserve the right to refuse any return requests once the order has been shipped. Only cancellations before shipment are eligible for refunds.

Returning Goods

If you wish to cancel your order before it has been shipped, please contact us immediately. Once verified, we will process the refund using the same payment method.

We recommend contacting us as soon as possible to ensure your cancellation is processed before shipment. No refunds will be issued for orders that have already been dispatched.

If the Goods were marked as a gift when purchased and shipped directly to You, You will receive a gift credit for the value of the returned Goods. Once we receive the returned Goods, a gift certificate will be sent to You.

If the Goods were not marked as a gift, the refund will be issued to the original purchaser.
